A word about us

Finding credible information about fitness and health at a single glance is difficult and if it is making you feel frustrated, here's a solution to that; Presenting an amazing website that is user-friendly and absolutely amazing.

We provide six different types of calculators which help to keep a track of your fitness on a regular basis and this will motivate you to maintain a healthy lifestyle.

Calculator Details

BMR

The BMR refers to the amount of energy your body needs to maintain homeostasis. BMR is the total number of calories that your body needs to perform basic, life-sustaining functions.

BMI

BMI is an estimate of body fat based on height and weight. It uses an equation to make an approximation to determine whether a person is at an unhealthy or healthy weight.

Calorie Intake

Calories are measures of energy. 1 calorie is equivalent to about 4.18 joules. Additionally, it's a good idea to divide the food calories between all meals in a healthy way.

Protien Intake

The protein calculator is an excellent tool for anyone who is interested in living a healthy lifestyle and eating a balanced diet. With its help, you'll be able to calculate the optimal protein intake for your weight and activity level.

Body Fat

Body fat is the total mass of fat divided by total body mass; body fat includes essential body fat and storage body fat.It is a good indicator of your body composition and indicates the amount of fat you have in your body.

Ideal weight

“What’s my ideal weight?” It’s a pretty normal question for most men and women. Knowing your ideal weight can help you make healthy choices for fitness, nutrition and lifestyle.
